About Us

Wellness Peak is a healthcare company founded in 2017. We strive to deliver to therapy patients a convenient, discreet, and engaging way to complete their therapy self-evaluations through our mobile-first web application.

We also provide an easy way for therapists to track all their patients in one place, their therapist dashboard. Through our fully-customizable therapy self-evaluations, therapists can truly customize each questionnaire to tailor a specific patient.
